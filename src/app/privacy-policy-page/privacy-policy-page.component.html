Welcome to Just Yams ("we", "our", or "us"). We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application and services.

Information We Collect

We do not collect any personal information that is not already publicly available on YouTube. Our app uses YouTube's OAuth 2.0 authentication process to access your YouTube account, and we only request the minimum permissions necessary to provide our services.

Specifically, our app may collect the following information:
  • YouTube Account Information: We may collect your YouTube channel information, username, and other public profile information associated with your YouTube account.
  • Video Data: Our app may retrieve video information, including video titles, descriptions, captions, and public statistics, from YouTube and store these in our database.
  • Caption Data: In order to improve our ability to caption videos accurately we may store captions which we generate on your behalf as well as any edits you make to those captions.

How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information solely to provide the services you have requested through our app. This includes uploading captions to YouTube videos and retrieving video data for your convenience. We do not use your information for any other purpose, such as marketing or advertising.

Data Security

We employ a robust and highly secure infrastructure to manage and protect your data. Our data storage solution is designed with a strong focus on security, utilizing advanced encryption techniques and access controls. All data is encrypted at rest and is transmitted only using HTTPS. This ensures that your information remains confidential and safeguarded against unauthorized access. Our chosen technology stack also incorporates rigorous monitoring and auditing processes to continuously assess and enhance security measures. Your data's safety and integrity are paramount to us, and we are committed to maintaining the highest standards of security throughout our operations.
